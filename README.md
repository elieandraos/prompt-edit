# Prompt Edit

A landing page for a creative AI toolkit aimed at video editors and content creators.

---

## Design

### Tone & Aesthetic

Modern, premium, and creative-focused. The design feels aspirational and professional without being corporate — the magenta accent keeps the energy high and the creative spirit intact. It's built for people who take their craft seriously but don't want to use tools that feel sterile or enterprise-grade.

### Color Palette

The foundation is a deep navy that feels immersive and cinematic. A single bold accent — a vibrant magenta-rose — punctuates interactive elements, calls to action, and highlights throughout the page. Sections transition between dark navy, deep purple-brown, and near-black, creating a sense of depth and richness as you scroll. White text at varying levels of opacity builds visual hierarchy without introducing additional colors.

### Typography

The typeface is Space Grotesk — a geometric sans-serif that reads as modern and slightly technical without feeling cold or clinical. Headings are large and confident, set with tight letter-spacing for a contemporary editorial feel. Body copy sits at a comfortable medium weight, while labels and supporting text are lighter, creating a clear and readable hierarchy throughout.

### Spacing & Layout

Vertical spacing between sections is generous — the design breathes rather than competes. Content is centered and constrained to comfortable reading widths so nothing feels overwhelming. Cards and containers use rounded corners throughout, giving the interface a soft, approachable quality despite the dark, dramatic palette.

### Details & Motion

The hero section features a diagonal gradient blending magenta into dark purple into navy — cinematic and bold. Surfaces throughout the page use a subtle glassmorphism treatment: translucent backgrounds with backdrop blur and thin semi-transparent borders that layer beautifully against the dark backdrop. Interactive elements transition smoothly and snappily — nothing jarring, everything intentional. Radial glows in section backgrounds echo the magenta accent color, adding warmth to otherwise dark surfaces.

### User Experience

Navigation stays unobtrusive — the header fades into content rather than competing with it. The hero search bar is the focal point of the landing experience: large, centered, and inviting, it immediately communicates the product's purpose and draws the user in to start exploring.

The pricing toggle between monthly and yearly billing is a smooth animated pill — a small but satisfying interaction that makes the choice feel effortless. The FAQ section uses an accordion pattern: answers are hidden by default and revealed with a gentle expand accompanied by a rotating chevron, keeping the page scannable without hiding information.

The AI tools section scrolls horizontally with gradient fade-outs at the edges, hinting at more content without cluttering the layout. Interactive states — hover, focus — are consistent and visible across the page, making the interface feel responsive and alive without being distracting.
